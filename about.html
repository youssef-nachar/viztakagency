About us

A story of good

Vistak was originated back in 2017 as a travel agency in Lebanon specializing in outbound tourism. It’s a pioneer agency offering wide array of services from tourism to investment and study abroad to immigration consultancy. Since its inception the vision was to make Vistak a global company. Over last half decade, company has expanded its wings by opening new branch offices in Lebanon, Australia, Azerbaijan, Georgia and Pakistan. Out company philosophy revolves around the phrase “Think globally, act locally” embedded in our value preposition which revolves around exceeding customer expectation, long term relationship and dealing ethically.
